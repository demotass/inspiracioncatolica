Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-01

This policy explains how we handle information in the “Catholic Inspiration” mobile app and on this informational website at https://demotass.github.io/inspiracioncatolica/. We designed the app with a simple principle: collect as little data as possible.

Summary

  • We do not create accounts or collect personally identifiable data in the app.
  • Favorites, personal quotes and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Notifications are local: scheduled and shown on your device; we do not send push from a server.
  • Text-to-speech is provided by your device OS; we do not send your text to our servers.
  • This website uses one technical cookie to remember your consent. Analytics are optional and only load if you allow them.

Data we process

In the mobile app

  • Local content: favorites, personal quotes and settings (including daily reminder) are stored in local device storage.
  • Local notifications: scheduling and firing occur on your device. Android may request permission to show notifications.
  • Text-to-speech (TTS): conversion is done by the device engine (e.g., iOS or Android). See their policies for details.

On this website

  • Necessary cookie: ic_consent to remember your cookie choices.
  • Optional analytics: only if you consent, we may load Google Analytics 4 or an equivalent solution. Data is used in aggregate to improve the site. You can change your choice on the Cookie Preferences page.

Legal bases (GDPR/EU)

  • For site analytics: consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR).
  • For app functionality (favorites, notifications): performance of the service requested by the user (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).

Retention

App data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Site analytics data (if any) is kept for the minimum required period (e.g., 14 months) as configured in the analytics tool.

Sharing and transfers

We do not sell or share your data. If you enable site analytics, the corresponding provider may process usage data under their terms. Where possible, we enable IP anonymization and retention controls.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EU/EEA), you may request access, rectification, deletion, objection or portability for identifiable data we process. As a rule we do not store personal data; still, you can contact us for any request.

Children

The app and site are intended for users 13+ (or the equivalent age in your country). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

Privacy contact email: demotass.dev@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. We will show the last updated date.
