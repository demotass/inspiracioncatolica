Prayer and contemplation

Catholic Inspiration is meant to be a small space of silence: read a quote, let it echo in your heart and turn it into prayer. The contemplative backgrounds and natural voice help you meditate at any moment of the day.

  • Read one quote slowly and in silence.
  • Save it to favorites to return later.
  • Enable a daily reminder to reserve a minute for prayer.
“Your word is a lamp for my steps” (cf. Ps 119)
